Game Intro

Goal: Explore the 32x32 dig site, search through sand, and find all 20 hidden diamond blocks.

Story: Your dog is buried somewhere near the starting area. Dig carefully, find the dog, and mine all 20 / 20 diamonds then You win!

Please use a browser that supports canvas.
FPS: -
Diamonds found: 0 / 20

Controls: Use W/A/S/D to move, click canvas to lock mouse, move mouse or use Q/E to look around, Press to Esc unlock mouse, Space to jump, F to add sand, R to dig sand, X for CHEAT MODE
