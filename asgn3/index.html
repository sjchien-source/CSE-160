Game Intro

Goal: Explore the 32x32 dig site, dig through sand, and uncover all 20 hidden diamond blocks.

Story: Your dog is buried somewhere near the starting area. Dig carefully, find your dog, then keep mining until the counter reaches 20 / 20. When all diamonds are found, the game says You win!

Mouse: Click the canvas to lock the mouse to the game. Move the mouse to look around. Press Esc to unlock.

FPS: -

Diamonds found: 0 / 20

Click the canvas to lock the mouse for FPS-style camera controls.

Please use a browser that supports canvas.

Controls: Use W/A/S/D to move, click canvas to lock mouse, move mouse or use Q/E to look around, Press to Esc unlock mouse, Space to jump, F to add sand, R to dig sand.
